In our continuing efforts to improve your concert experience United Palace Concerts is pleased to offer our patrons Valet Parking Service!
No more stress over long lines and wasted time parking your car, simply drive up to the front entrance of the United Palace located at 4140 Broadway between 175th and 176th Street and receive your valet ticket. Once you receive your valet ticket all you need to do is enjoy the concert!

The valet service will handle the parking of your vehicle and return your vehicle upon the completion of the United Palace Concert performance.



United Palace Concerts is not responsible for vehicle damage, lost or stolen property.
