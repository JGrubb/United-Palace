Welcome to New York's Premier Concert Venue, United Palace Concerts where the greatest live music is performed at the greatest music venue in NYC. As you enter the Palace you will immediately feel the energy from the magnificent design and historic ambiance, new world acoustics and spectacular architecture. United Palace Concerts is located on Broadway in Washington Heights, just a few blocks from the GWB and minutes from all major public transportation. The United Palace Concerts is the most spectacular live concert venue in NYC and hosts world renowned rock and roll bands. We feature performances from classical rock to pop, and everything in between. United Palace Concerts inspires artists to perform at their very best while providing concert goers with a plush setting in which to enjoy their favorite music. For that unforgettable musical experience that you and your friends will talk about for years to come, make United Palace Concerts your destination.
